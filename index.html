WebGL Sky Driver 🛫✨ Help

🎮 Features

  • Procedural track generation with a deterministic seed.
  • Two camera styles: Dynamic (swings on turns) and Static (fixed chase cam).
  • Simple physics: Gravity, grounded jumping, and lateral steering.
  • Minimalist WebGL rendering with a 2D Canvas HUD for the score.
  • Responsive canvas that resizes to the viewport.
